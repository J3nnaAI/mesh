# Vision

**J3nna Mesh, powered by the J3nna Integration Protocol (JIP), turns your entire digital infrastructure into a single, active workspace.**

It replaces isolated tools with a distributed team of specialized agents that work across your computers, services, and networks. They don't just execute tasks — they **synthesize data and context from every corner of your environment** to deliver results that are smarter than any single tool could produce.

## Our northstar

Seamless integration and interoperability, where **AI is a first-class citizen in every application, every integration, every device** — an agent in a broad and highly capable ecosystem.

## The shift

Today, software is a drawer full of disconnected tools. You are the integration layer: you carry context from one app to the next, copy results between services, and keep the whole picture in your head. The agent era is making this worse, not better — every assistant becomes many agents, and without a common fabric they end up siloed inside whichever cloud spawned them.

J3nna Mesh is the fabric. Identity, discovery, capability, and trust become part of the substrate itself, so any agent — anywhere in your environment — can find any other, prove who it is, and call what it's allowed to. The integration layer stops being *you* and becomes the mesh.

- **Your infrastructure, as one workspace.** Computers, services, and networks present a single surface that
  agents operate across, not a pile of tabs and APIs you stitch together by hand.
- **A team, not a tool.** Specialized agents collaborate — discovering each other, exposing and calling each
  other's capabilities, sharing context — so the whole is smarter than any part.
- **Synthesis over execution.** The value isn't running a task in isolation; it's combining what every part
  of your environment knows into an answer no single tool could reach.
- **AI as a first-class citizen.** Every application, integration, and device can *be* an agent and *use*
  agents — a peer in the ecosystem, not a feature bolted onto one app.

And it stays **yours**: decentralized and governed, with no broker on the data path and trust you issue and revoke yourself. That governance is what makes the vision safe to actually run — see **[WHY.md](WHY.md)** for why the agent layer must stay open and owned by the people who run it.
